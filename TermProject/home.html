Super Smash Bros first came out in 1999. The newest entry came out in 2018. 19 years of gaming.

The series first started with a roster of 12 fighters from various Nintendo franchises.

The roster now has 74 fighters and no longer has just Nintendo characters. One such fighter is Snake from Konami's Metal Gear series..

The series has a player base of varying ages and is a game that transcends generations.

The game has a competitive scene if one wants to tryhard. It is also a game that you can play with family for fun
